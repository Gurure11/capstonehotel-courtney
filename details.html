About Us


Welcome to Capestone Hotel, your premier destination for an unforgettable stay in the heart of Harare, Zimbabwe's vibrant Central
Business District. Situated just minutes away from the airport, a plethora of cultural attractions, and the city's major facilities,
Capestone Hotel offers a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and luxury.


Our Location


Nestled in the bustling CBD of Harare, Capestone Hotel provides easy access to key landmarks and entertainment options. Whether you're here
for business or leisure, you'll find our location to be the ideal starting point for your Harare adventure. Explore the nearby zoo,
catch a movie at the cinema, or discover the rich cultural heritage of Zimbabwe, all within easy reach.



Accommodations


At Capestone Hotel, we take pride in offering spacious and well-appointed accommodations that cater to both business travelers and
tourists. With over 30 comfortable rooms designed for couples, you'll find the perfect retreat after a day of exploring the city.
Our rooms are equipped with modern amenities to ensure your stay is as enjoyable as possible.





Recreational Amenities



Relax and rejuvenate at our beautiful swimming pool area, perfect for unwinding under the Zimbabwean sun. For those who love the sizzle
of a barbecue, our braai area is at your disposal, creating opportunities for memorable evenings with friends and family.



Dining and Entertainment


Savor exquisite cuisine at our on-site restaurant, where our talented chefs prepare a delectable array of local and international dishes.
The bar offers a diverse selection of beverages, making it an ideal spot to unwind with a drink in hand.



Our Commitment



At Capestone Hotel, we are committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring that every aspect of your stay is truly memorable.
Our dedicated staff is here to assist you with any request, ensuring your comfort and satisfaction throughout your visit.




Whether you're visiting Harare for business, leisure, or a special occasion, Capestone Hotel is your home away from home. Experience
the warmth of Zimbabwean hospitality, the convenience of our location, and the comfort of our accommodations. We look forward to hosting
you and making your stay in Harare an unforgettable one.
